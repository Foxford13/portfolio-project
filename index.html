About me

A couple of years ago I made friends with designers and programmers in an online forum. After being shown some of their work, being naturally curious, I decided to learn a bit of programming. Realising that it's one of a few things that I have ever done that I couldn’t be pulled away from, I decided to make it my career. That has led me to GA WDI course where I had the chance to work with more complex technologies that I have never experienced before. With experience gathered during the course and a lot of research of the industry, I was reinforced in my conviction that this is a right path for me. Now I am looking for a team that would help me grow and would allow me to give something back to the community.
